How to Play


  • 1. Once the game starts, a random dish is chosen from a pre-set selection.

  • 2. You will have to guess what this dish is after 3 rounds!

  • 3. Don't worry, each round you will be given a chance to ask for a hint - 3 hints in total.

  • Tip: Click on the food photos to eliminate options based on the hints you have received.
